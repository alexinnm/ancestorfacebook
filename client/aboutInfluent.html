Insights from the front line of special operations, emergency medicine, and innovation management

Influent Health is the result of a blending of diverse perspectives from our expert team. By combining leadership insights of three different fields, we bring a unique set of tools to the complex problems of healthcare.

Alex Rich is completing an MPH at Yale University in May of 2017. He is a former Major and Senior Pilot in US Air Force Special Operations Command. He was trained as an instructor pilot and Flight Safety Officer and spent several years helping manage the safety and mishap investigation program of a Wing operating $3.6 billion in KC-135 aircraft. Alex was a finalist at the 2016 MIT Hacking Healthcare Grand Hack. He spent the summer of 2106 at the Kaiser Permanente Northern California office of Quality and Operations Support consulting on a user interface redesign. He holds a B.S. in Aerospace Engineering from Virginia Tech and an MBA from Oklahoma State University.

Jean Zheng, PhD is the former Engineering Director at the Yale Center for Biomedical and Interventional Technology. She has managed 50+ clinical-led medical device projects and was the catalyst a lead of the first Healthcare Hackathons at Yale University. She led a team that won the Brigham Innovation Hub Prize at MIT Hacking Medicine’s Brigham & Women’s Hospital Healthcare Hackathon in 2014. She also won the National Science Foundation Graduate Research Fellowship award 2008-2011. In addition to bringing a wealth of experience, perspective and connections, Jean holds an M.S./PH.D. in Mechanical Engineering from Yale University and a BS in Mechanical Engineering from MIT.

Dave McCredie is completing an MPH at the University of Michigan in May of 2017. Dave is a former Captain and Evaluator Combat Systems Operator in US Air Force Special Operations Command. Dave holds a BS in Chemistry from the University of Texas and a Masters in Foreign Affairs from the University of Oklahoma.

John Cronin is an internal consultant at an academic medical center and an emergency department nurse with 10 years of experience. He has worked at 3 of the 5 largest trauma centers in the US and is medevac qualified. He holds an MPH from Yale University.
