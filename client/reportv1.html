    General Incident Background

    Bringing the roots of Aviation Safety to American Healthcare!

    NearMiss Health is the dream of Clinical experts, Biostatisticians, Healthcare Administrators, and an Air Force Pilot and Crash Investigator. It seeks to bring the safety gains of easy, online, anonymous reporting of near misses realized by US Commercial aviation to healthcare.

    Take the journey with us! Together, we're going save a LOT of lives..

    The NMH team is currently developing the back end and database structure necessary for the platform's first pilot deployment

    We'd love to hear your perspective, gather your feedback, and enage with your hospital. Please reach out to us at nearmisshealth.com and let's start the conversation!
